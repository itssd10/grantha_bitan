Charitraheen

~By Sarat Chandra chattopadhyay

Charitraheen by Sarat Chandra chattopadhyay tells a story of Sabitri, a beautiful woman and widow, who has been thrown out from her husband's home by her in-laws driven to work as a maidservant in a youth hostel, where she falls in love with her master. With a publication of this novel Chattopadhyay established himself as a supporter of women's rights.
